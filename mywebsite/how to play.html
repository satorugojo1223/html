HOW TO ACTUALLY PLAY?

For your first day in Minecraft, the goal is simply to survive the night. Begin by punching trees to collect wood, then use it to craft a crafting table, and from there, create basic wooden tools. With a wooden pickaxe, you can mine cobblestone to upgrade to more durable stone tools, and importantly, make a furnace. As dusk approaches, either dig a hole into the side of a hill or build a small shelter to protect yourself from hostile monsters. If you find three sheep, you can also craft a bed to skip the night entirely.
