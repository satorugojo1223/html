Satoru Gojo is a prominent character in the Jujutsu Kaisen series, known as the strongest jujutsu sorcerer. He possesses immense power derived from his unique abilities, including the Limitless Cursed Technique and the Six Eyes, which grant him unparalleled control over space and cursed energy. Beyond his strength, Gojo is known for his charismatic personality, playful demeanor, and strategic mind in battle. He serves as a teacher at Tokyo Metropolitan咒术高等専門学校 (Tokyo Metropolitan Jujutsu Technical High School), guiding and mentoring the next generation of sorcerers. Gojo's Limitless Cursed Technique allows him to manipulate space, distorting, expanding, and controlling it at will, making him a formidable opponent. The Six Eyes, another of his innate abilities, grants him heightened perception, allowing him to analyze and manipulate cursed energy with exceptional precision. His combat style is a blend of raw power and strategic thinking, often targeting enemy weaknesses with pinpoint accuracy and incredible speed. Despite his power and confidence, Gojo is also portrayed as having a complex personality, displaying both humor and seriousness, and caring deeply for his students. He is a complex and compelling character, revered for his strength and admired for his charisma.

Satoru Gojo is a tall, striking individual with distinctive white, spiky hair and vibrant blue eyes.
