Satoru Gojo is a special grade Jujutsu Sorcerer and widely regarded as the strongest in the world. He is known for his unique abilities, particularly his inherited techniques of Limitless and the Six Eyes, according to Beebom and the Jujutsu Kaisen Wiki. The Six Eyes allow him to see and manipulate cursed energy with incredible precision. Gojo works as a teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High and is actively involved in training and protecting young sorcerers. He is a descendant of the powerful Sugawara Michizane clan.
